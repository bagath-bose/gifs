chemistry

  1. Chemistry is the scientific study of the properties and behavior of matter.
  2. It is a natural science that covers the elements that make up matter to the compounds composed of atoms, molecules and ions
  3. their composition, structure, properties, behavior and the changes they undergo during a reaction with other substances.
  4. In the scope of its subject, chemistry occupies an intermediate position between physics and biology
  5. It is sometimes called the central science because it provides a foundation for understanding both basic and applied scientific disciplines at a fundamental level
