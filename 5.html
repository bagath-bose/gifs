smart phone

  1. A smartphone is a portable device that combines mobile telephone and computing functions into one unit.
  2. They are distinguished from feature phones by their stronger hardware capabilities and extensive mobile operating systems
  3. Smartphones typically contain a number of metal–oxide–semiconductor (MOS) integrated circuit (IC) chips, include various sensors that can be leveraged by pre-included and third-party
  4. Early smartphones were marketed primarily towards the enterprise market, attempting to bridge the functionality of standalone personal digital assistant
  5. n June 1999 Qualcomm released the "pdQ Smartphone", a CDMA digital PCS smartphone with an integrated Palm PDA and Internet connectivity
